## How to read this data room

1. **Start with 01 (Executive Summary)** for the thesis in two pages, then **02 (Pitch Deck)** for the full narrative.
2. **Product diligence:** 03 → 04 → 05 → 06. The architecture map (05) and agent spec (06) are written directly from the working codebase, not from aspiration.
3. **Commercial diligence:** 07 → 10 → 11 → 12.
4. **Financial diligence:** 08 → 09.
5. **Legal / structural diligence:** 13 → 14. If you are evaluating the relationship between Bey Group International (BGI), BGI Fund I, and FundExecs Technologies, read 14 first.

## Notes and disclaimers

- **Forward-looking statements.** Documents 08, 09, 10, and 12 contain projections and estimates. They are illustrative planning models based on stated assumptions, not guarantees of performance. Actual results will differ.
- **Product status.** FundExecs OS is in alpha. Sections describing shipped functionality reflect the state of the codebase as of July 2026; sections describing roadmap items are plans, not commitments.
- **No offer.** Nothing in this data room constitutes an offer to sell securities. Any investment would be made only through definitive legal documentation, subject to investor qualification and securities counsel review.
- **Separate entities.** FundExecs Technologies (the software company) is distinct from Bey Group International and BGI Fund I. The BGI Founding Capital Circle is a separate instrument relating to the BGI platform, not equity in FundExecs Technologies. See document 14.
