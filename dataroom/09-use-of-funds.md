## 3. Milestones this capital reaches

**Within 6 months of close**
- Three-graph query layer (`/graph/*`) + visualizations shipped
- 3–5 external design partners live (beyond BGI Fund I), weekly feedback cadence
- Email/webhook/event automation triggers live; first org-memory (Brain recall v2) increment

**Within 12 months**
- All four hubs feature-complete for the emerging-manager workflow (raise + pipeline + diligence + reporting)
- Public beta; 25+ paying organizations; institutional repricing validated with at least two Scale-tier contracts
- SOC 2 Type I attained

**Within 18–24 months (Series A position)**
- ~$1M+ ARR run-rate, NRR ≥110%
- Marketplace v1 live with first matched transactions
- Proactive agent proposals shipped (the Phase-3 vision demonstrably working)
- Clean data room for an $8–10M Series A

## 4. What we will *not* spend on

- Paid acquisition at scale — the wedge is founder network, salons, referrals, and product-led entry; CAC discipline is a feature of the plan, not a constraint.
- Speculative on-chain deployment — the trust layer stays on the internal auditable ledger until counsel and customer demand justify anchoring (document 13).
- Office/real-estate overhead — remote-first.

## 5. Sensitivity

- **If the round closes at $1.5M:** drop one engineering hire and the marketplace v1 scope; hold the 12-month milestones; runway ~18 months to the same beta + 25-org proof point.
- **If the round is oversubscribed to $3.5M:** pull forward the AI engineer and the enterprise tier (SSO/SAML, governance pack), targeting $1.5M+ ARR run-rate at month 24.
