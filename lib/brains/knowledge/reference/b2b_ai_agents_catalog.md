# 100 AI Agents for B2B Companies — Reference Catalog

> Shared reference knowledge for the growth, marketing, SEO, and operations
> Brains. A working vocabulary of AI agent archetypes across the B2B revenue
> engine — lead research and scraping, qualification and scoring, outreach and
> copywriting, content and SEO, social and prospecting, conversion, and customer
> feedback. Use it to name, scope, and assemble agent-driven workflows.
>
> Transcribed verbatim from the source catalog; a few archetypes intentionally
> recur across categories (e.g., LinkedIn Prospector, CTA Optimizer), reflecting
> that the same agent pattern shows up in more than one part of the funnel.
